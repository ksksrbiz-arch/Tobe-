- `castle/tower-base.glb`, `castle/tower-mid.glb`, `castle/tower-roof.glb`
  — Castle Kit (kept in their own folder with their own `Textures/colormap.png`,
  since the Castle Kit's colormap differs from the Pirate Kit's)

## Polygonal Mind (<https://github.com/ToxSam/cc0-models-Polygonal-Mind>)

- `crystal-cluster.glb` — "Crystal_ClusterSurrounded" from the Crystal Crossroads
  collection. Self-contained (texture embedded in the .glb). The black
  inverted-hull outline shell that ships with it is hidden at load time, and its
  base is normalized to the origin so it can be placed on the island rim.

Used in `components/MagicBook3D.tsx` (the homepage hero animation).
